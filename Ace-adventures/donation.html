Lab Lovers Rescue

Lab Lovers Rescue is a not-for-profit volunteer dog rescue organization serving Rochester, NY and surrounding areas. Our focus is on Labrador Retrievers, however we will help with other breeds and animals when space allows.

About Me

Cookie is a 67 lb., one year old Chocolate Lab and has the energy of a young dog. She is smart and quick to learn, energetic, and sweet. She'd love a home with another dog with the same energy level that will play with her.

Spayed, up to date on vaccinations, and microchipped, Cookie came to Rochester, NY from South Carolina. She is completely house trained and crate trained.

She was good with her foster's energetic 5 year old grandson. During transport she was awesome with other dogs, cats and kids.

Cookie loves fetch and returns the ball to you. She knows sit, paw, down, stay (for short time) and is learning "wait".

A great cuddler, Cookie enjoys sharing the couch with her foster mom. On occasion she does jump up on people, but not all the time. Cookie is very strong on a leash.
